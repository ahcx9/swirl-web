Frequently asked questions

How do I update a digital menu?

You can easily update a digital menu through a management platform, allowing you to change items, prices, or promotions instantly.

Do I need an app to access a digital menu?

Some digital menus are accessible via a mobile app, while others use QR codes or web links, so customers can view them without downloading anything.

Is it possible to integrate digital menus with online ordering?

Yes, many digital menu systems integrate with online ordering platforms, making it seamless for customers to place orders directly from the menu.

What are the benefits of using a digital menu?

Digital menus offer flexibility, allowing for quick updates, easy customization, and a more engaging customer experience. They can also reduce printing costs and streamline operations.
